Bio



To create exceptional music that is able to sway the crowed and fill listeners with various emotions great artist must speak from a place that's genuine and relatable. These are the two amendments that define Illie. As a D.C., Maryland and Virginia (DMV) native, Illie started his career recording in the basement of his parents home were he studied the greats (e.g., Jay-z and Lil Wayne) to help hone his craft.

After graduating from Hampton University in the spring of 2014, and becoming introduced to the "real world," Illie decided to become more active in perusing his hobby as a means to make a secondary income. In summer of 2015, Illie became acquainted with his engineer, mentor and former Def Jam A&R Vince aka V Da Don who has assisted in further molding Illie into becoming a standout performer. His Debut Mixtape "#NowThatsILL" is a title meant to convey the wide range and diverse ability of Illie as a performer. On this mixtape Illie concurred various musical genres which include Classical Hip-Hop, Rock, EDM, Trap and R&B.

The Future looks bright for Illie. He is a true artist that is on the rise from being a local talent to a mainstream super star with a lot of planned endeavors in the works to take not only the music industry by storm, but also the world. Watch out!
